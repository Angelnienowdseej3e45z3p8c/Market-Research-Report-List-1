<p><p>Polyurethane foam insulation market is classified into three main types: Flexible foam, Spray foam, and Rigid foam. Flexible foam is used for applications like cushioning, carpet underlay, and packaging. Spray foam is commonly used for insulation purposes in buildings and appliances due to its ability to fill gaps and create a seamless barrier. Rigid foam, on the other hand, is ideal for insulation in walls, roofs, and floors due to its high thermal resistance. Each type serves specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
