<p><p>Primer Filler market is divided into two main types: One-Component Primer Filler and Two-Component Primer Filler. One-Component Primer Filler is a ready-to-use product that does not require any mixing before application, making it convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, Two-Component Primer Filler consists of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application, offering better adhesion and durability. Both types of primer filler are essential for preparing surfaces before painting, providing a smooth and uniform base for the topcoat.</p></p>
