<p><ul><li>Regular Router</li><li>Core Router</li><li>Edge Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular routers are typically used in households or small businesses to connect multiple devices to the internet. Core routers are used by internet service providers to direct traffic between different networks. Edge routers are used at the edge of a network to connect the network to external networks or the internet. Each type of router serves a specific purpose in managing and directing network traffic, with regular routers focusing on individual device connections, core routers handling high volumes of traffic between networks, and edge routers connecting networks to external sources.</p></p>
