<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type (Bulk Piezo, Thin Film Piezo)</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the inkjet print heads market, there are two main types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric print heads can be further categorized as bulk piezo or thin film piezo. Bulk piezo technology involves a larger piezoelectric element that generates pressure to create ink droplets, while thin film piezo technology uses a smaller, more precise element. On the other hand, thermal print heads use heat to vaporize ink and create droplets, offering a different method of inkjet printing.</p></p>
