<p><p>Glass free 3D TV technology is categorized into two main types: Active 3D TV and Passive 3D TV. In the Active 3D TV market, viewers wear special glasses that synchronize with the TV screen to create a three-dimensional effect. On the other hand, Passive 3D TV utilizes polarized glasses that separate the images for each eye, resulting in a similar 3D viewing experience. Both types offer a glasses-free viewing experience, but each has its own distinct technology and market appeal.</p></p>
