<p><p>Power over Ethernet (POE) lighting market consists of two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs, and Powered Device Controllers & ICs. Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs are used in devices that supply power to the POE lighting system, such as switches and injectors. Powered Device Controllers & ICs are utilized in devices that receive power from the POE lighting system, such as LED fixtures and sensors. These components play a crucial role in enabling efficient and effective deployment of POE lighting systems.</p></p>
