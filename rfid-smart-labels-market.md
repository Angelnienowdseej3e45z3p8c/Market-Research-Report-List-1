<p><strong>The RFID Smart Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Tags</li><li>Active Tags</li><li>Battery Assisted Passive Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive tags in the RFID smart labels market are cost-effective and rely on an external RFID reader for power. Active tags have their own power source and can transmit signals over longer distances. Battery assisted passive tags combine the benefits of both passive and active tags by using a small battery to extend the read range of passive tags. Each type of tag caters to different needs in terms of cost, range, and functionality, making them suitable for a variety of applications in industries like retail, healthcare, and logistics.</p></p>
