<p><ul><li>UV (i-line)</li><li>DUV (KrF, ArF Dry, ArFi)</li><li>EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photolithography equipment market comprises UV (i-line), DUV (KrF, ArF Dry, ArFi), and EUV machines. UV photolithography uses ultraviolet light with a longer wavelength, while DUV machines utilize shorter wavelengths like KrF and ArF Dry. The latest technology, EUV lithography, employs extreme ultraviolet light for higher resolution and efficiency. Each type caters to specific requirements in semiconductor manufacturing, with EUV marking the latest advancement in lithography technology for cutting-edge microchip production.</p></p>
