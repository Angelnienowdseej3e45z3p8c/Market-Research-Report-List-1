<p><p>Precast concrete beams are commonly categorized into two main types: frame beams and non-frame beams. Frame beams are designed to be used as part of a structural frame, providing support and stability to the overall structure. Non-frame beams, on the other hand, are used for various non-structural applications such as decorative elements or barriers. Both types of beams offer unique characteristics and benefits depending on the specific requirements of the project they are being used for.</p></p>
