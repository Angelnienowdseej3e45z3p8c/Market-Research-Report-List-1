<p><p>Refrigerator trucks are divided into two main types based on their temperature control capabilities: Single Temperature Type and Multi-Temperature Type. Single Temperature Type trucks are designed to transport goods that require a consistent temperature throughout the entire truck compartment. On the other hand, Multi-Temperature Type trucks have separate compartments with different temperature controls, allowing for the transport of goods requiring varying temperatures. Both types cater to different industries and the specific needs of businesses involved in the transportation of temperature-sensitive goods.</p></p>
