<p><p>Bio-derived enzymes for detergents market can be categorized into five main types: amylases, cellulases, lipases, peroxidases, and pullulanase. Amylases are used to break down starches, cellulases break down cellulose fibers, lipases break down fats and oils, peroxidases break down stubborn stains, and pullulanase break down pullulan. These enzymes are derived from natural sources like plants and microorganisms, making them eco-friendly alternatives to traditional chemical-based detergents. They offer effective cleaning power while being biodegradable and non-toxic.</p></p>
