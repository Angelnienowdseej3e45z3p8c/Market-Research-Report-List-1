<p><p>Fume chambers are a type of containment device used to protect against exposure to harmful fumes, gases, and particles. Two main types of fume chambers are ductless fume chambers and ducted fume chambers. </p><p>Ductless fume chambers utilize built-in filters to remove contaminants from the air before recirculating it back into the room, making them more portable and cost-effective. </p><p>On the other hand, ducted fume chambers are connected to an external ventilation system, allowing for the direct release of contaminated air outside the building.</p></p>
