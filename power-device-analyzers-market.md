<p><p>Power device analyzers are specialized tools used to measure and analyze electrical parameters in power systems. These analyzers are categorized into two main types: AC and DC analyzers. AC analyzers are used to measure parameters in alternating current systems, such as voltage, current, power factor, and harmonics. DC analyzers, on the other hand, are designed for direct current systems and are used to measure parameters like voltage, current, resistance, and capacitance. Both types of analyzers play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and reliability of power systems.</p></p>
