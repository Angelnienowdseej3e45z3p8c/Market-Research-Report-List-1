<p><p>Disposable correction tape is available in two main types: paper-based and polyester film-based. Paper-based correction tape is made with a thin strip of paper coated with white correction material that can be applied to handwritten or printed text. Polyester film-based correction tape uses a clear, durable film with a layer of white correction material on top. The paper-based tape is more commonly used in offices and schools, while the polyester film-based tape is preferred for more precise and professional corrections.</p></p>
