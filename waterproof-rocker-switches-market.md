<p><p>Waterproof rocker switches are available in two main types in the market, single-pole and multi-pole. Single-pole waterproof rocker switches have a single switch contact that opens and closes a single circuit, making them ideal for simple on/off applications. On the other hand, multi-pole waterproof rocker switches have multiple switch contacts that can control multiple circuits simultaneously, allowing for more complex control options. Both types of switches are designed to withstand moisture and water exposure, making them suitable for outdoor or marine applications.</p></p>
