<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Three-Phase Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core-Type Three-Phase Transformer(Single Core,Two Cores)</li><li>Shell-Type Three-Phase Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core-Type Three-Phase Transformers are available in two types: Single Core and Two Cores. Single Core transformers have all three phases wound on a single magnetic core, while Two Cores have two separate cores for each phase. These transformers are known for their high efficiency and compact design. On the other hand, Shell-Type Three-Phase Transformers have three separate magnetic cores surrounded by windings. They are popular for their high power capacity and ability to handle load fluctuations efficiently.</p></p>
