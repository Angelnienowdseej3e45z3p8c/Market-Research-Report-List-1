<p><strong>The Gas Wall-hung Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant Type</li><li>Volumetric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas wall-hung boilers come in two main types: Instant Type and Volumetric Type. Instant Type boilers provide hot water on demand, heating water as it passes through the unit. These boilers are ideal for households with fluctuating hot water needs. Volumetric Type boilers store hot water in a tank and maintain a constant supply of hot water ready for use. These boilers are suitable for households requiring a consistent supply of hot water without the need for waiting for heating.</p></p>
