<p><p>SMP Based Elastic Adhesives come in two main market types: One Component and Two Component. One Component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick applications. Two Component adhesives require mixing of separate components before use, allowing for more control over setting times and bond strength.
