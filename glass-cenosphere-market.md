<p><p>The Glass Cenosphere market can be categorized into two main types based on the density of the cenospheres - Floating Beads and Sinking Beads. Floating Beads are lightweight cenospheres that have a lower density and tend to float on water, making them suitable for applications such as lightweight fillers in construction and insulation materials.
