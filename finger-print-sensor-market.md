<p><p>Fingerprint sensors can be categorized into different types based on their technology. Capacitive sensors use an electrical current to detect fingerprint patterns, while ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint. RF sensors use radio frequency signals to read fingerprints. Apart from these, there are other types of fingerprint sensors in the market, such as optical sensors. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, catering to the diverse needs of various industries and applications.</p></p>
