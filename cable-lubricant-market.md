<p><ul><li>Heavy-duty Lubricant</li><li>Cable Blowing Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable Lubricant market can be divided into two main types: Heavy-duty Lubricant and Cable Blowing Lubricant. Heavy-duty Lubricants are used for demanding cable installations where high friction is expected. These types of lubricants provide superior lubrication and reduce the risk of cable damage during installation. On the other hand, Cable Blowing Lubricants are specifically designed for use in cable blowing machines to help facilitate the smooth installation of cables through conduits. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in ensuring seamless and efficient cable installations.</p></p>
