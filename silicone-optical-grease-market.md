<p><p>Silicone optical grease is available in three main types: white, gray, and black. White silicone grease is commonly used for sealing and lubricating optical components. Gray silicone grease is typically used for thermal coupling and heat sink applications. Black silicone grease is often used for light-absorbing purposes in optical devices. Each type offers specific properties and benefits to meet different needs in the optical industry, such as thermal conductivity, light absorption, and lubrication.</p></p>
