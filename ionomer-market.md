<p><ul><li>Ethylene Acrylic Acid (EAA) Copolymer</li><li>Ethylene Methacrylic Acid (EMMA) Copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ionomer markets are divided into two types: Ethylene Acrylic Acid (EAA) Copolymer and Ethylene Methacrylic Acid (EMAA) Copolymer. EAA copolymers are known for their excellent adhesion properties, toughness, and clarity, making them ideal for applications in food packaging, automotive parts, and consumer goods. On the other hand, EMAA copolymers offer superior heat resistance and chemical resistance, making them suitable for use in high-performance coatings, films, and adhesives. Both types of ionomers play a crucial role in various industries due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
