<p><p>Biopolyethylene is classified into different market types based on their density and branching characteristics. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) is a rigid, tough plastic with a high strength-to-density ratio. Linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) is flexible and has high impact strength and puncture resistance. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) is a more flexible plastic with high transparency and moisture barrier properties. These different types of biopolyethylene cater to various applications and industries due to their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
