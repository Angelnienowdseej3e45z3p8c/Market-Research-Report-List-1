<p><strong>The Microwave Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Radar</li><li>Doppler-effect</li><li>Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave</li><li>Ultra-wideband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microwave motion sensor market types include pulsed radar, Doppler-effect, frequency modulated continuous wave, and ultra-wideband. Pulsed radar sensors operate by emitting short bursts of microwave energy and measuring the reflected signal. Doppler-effect sensors detect motion based on changes in frequency caused by moving objects. Frequency modulated continuous wave sensors use a continuous transmission of varying frequencies to detect motion. Ultra-wideband sensors transmit short pulses of energy across a wide range of frequencies for precise motion detection and tracking.</p></p>
