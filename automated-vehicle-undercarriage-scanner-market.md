<p><p>Fixed automated vehicle undercarriage scanners are permanently installed at a specific location, typically at checkpoints or entrances where vehicles pass through. Mobile automated vehicle undercarriage scanners, on the other hand, are portable and can be moved to different locations as needed. They are typically used in situations where a fixed scanner may not be feasible or practical.
