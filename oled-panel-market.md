<p><ul><li>Active Matrix</li><li>Passive Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED panel market is divided into two main types: active matrix and passive matrix. Active matrix OLED panels are more advanced and commonly used in modern devices due to their ability to individually control each pixel, resulting in higher resolution and better image quality. Passive matrix OLED panels are simpler and cheaper, but they have slower response times and lower resolution. Both types play a key role in the OLED panel market, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
