<p><p>UV fluorescent pigments are classified into three main market types: thermoset type, thermoplastic type, and others. Thermoset type UV fluorescent pigments are characterized by their ability to cure or set into a permanent form when subjected to heat or radiation, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and chemical resistance. Thermoplastic type UV fluorescent pigments, on the other hand, remain pliable when heated and hardened when cooled, allowing for easy molding and reshaping. Other types of UV fluorescent pigments may include specialty formulations with unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
