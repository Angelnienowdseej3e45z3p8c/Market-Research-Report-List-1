<p><p>Magnesium Oxide Substrates are categorized into three types based on thickness: Thin (below 8 mm), Medium (8-15 mm), and Thick (above 15 mm). Thin substrates are commonly used in lightweight applications that require flexibility and portability. Medium substrates are ideal for general-purpose applications where a balance between thickness and weight is necessary. Thick substrates are suitable for heavy-duty applications requiring high durability and strength. Each type caters to specific needs in different industries such as electronics, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
