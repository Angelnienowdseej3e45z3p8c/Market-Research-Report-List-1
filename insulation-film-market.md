<p><p>Insulation films are categorized into different types based on the materials used. These include polypropylene insulation film, polyethylene insulation film, polyamide insulation film, and others. Polypropylene insulation film is known for its high thermal resistance and low moisture absorption. Polyethylene insulation film is popular for its flexibility and moisture resistance. Polyamide insulation film offers excellent mechanical strength and temperature resistance. The "others" category may include insulation films made from materials such as polyester or polycarbonate with unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
