<p><ul><li>High Temperature Austenite Phase</li><li>Low Temperature Martensite Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fe based shape memory alloys have two main types in the market: high temperature austenite phase and low temperature martensite phase. The high temperature austenite phase is known for its ability to return to its original shape at high temperatures, making it ideal for applications where heat resistance is important. On the other hand, the low temperature martensite phase exhibits shape memory properties at lower temperatures, making it suitable for use in a wider range of environments. Both types offer unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
