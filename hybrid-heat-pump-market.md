<p><ul><li>Compression Heat Pump</li><li>Absorption Heat Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid heat pumps combine the technology of compression heat pumps and absorption heat pumps to provide efficient and cost-effective heating and cooling solutions. Compression heat pumps use electricity to compress and expand refrigerant gases to transfer heat, while absorption heat pumps use a combination of heat and a refrigerant absorber to achieve the same goal. By integrating these two technologies, hybrid heat pumps offer a versatile system that can adapt to different environmental conditions and energy sources, making them a popular choice in the market.</p></p>
