<p><p>Wood doors market consists of various types, with three main categories being Solid Wood Door, Wood Composite Door, and Molded Door. Solid Wood Doors are made entirely of natural wood, offering a traditional and high-quality option. Wood Composite Doors are made from a combination of wood fibers and other materials, providing a more affordable alternative with added durability. Molded Doors are created by pressing together wood fibers under high pressure, resulting in a cost-effective option that mimics the look of real wood.</p></p>
