<p><ul><li>Silicone Based Polyimide Tape</li><li>Acrylic Based Polyimide Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide tape is available in two main types: silicone-based and acrylic-based. Silicone-based polyimide tape offers high temperature resistance and excellent electrical insulation properties, making it ideal for applications in electronics and automotive industries. On the other hand, acrylic-based polyimide tape provides good adhesion and conformability, making it suitable for general purpose masking and protection tasks. Both types of polyimide tape offer superior performance in high temperature and harsh environments, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
