<p><p>Connected street lights can be categorized into two main types: wired (Power Line Communication) and wireless markets. In the wired market, street lights are connected through existing power lines, allowing for easy and reliable communication between each light. On the other hand, in the wireless market, street lights are connected using wireless technology, such as radio waves or Wi-Fi. This provides a more flexible and adaptable solution for connecting street lights, particularly in areas where laying down wired infrastructure may be challenging or costly.</p></p>
