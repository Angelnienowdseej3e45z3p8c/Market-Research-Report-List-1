<p><p>The Ultra-High Voltage (UHV) market consists of two main types: AC UHV and DC UHV. AC UHV technology involves transmitting electricity using alternating current at extremely high voltages, while DC UHV technology involves transmitting electricity using direct current at the same high voltages. AC UHV is more commonly used for long-distance power transmission, while DC UHV is preferred for transmitting large amounts of power over long distances with lower energy losses. Both types play a crucial role in modern power transmission systems.</p></p>
