<p><p>Truck Shock Absorber Market has two main types: hydraulic pressure and pneumatic. Hydraulic pressure shock absorbers use hydraulic fluid to dampen the impact of bumps on the road, providing a smoother ride. Pneumatic shock absorbers use compressed air to provide support and stability to the vehicle. Both types aim to improve the overall performance and comfort of trucks by absorbing and minimizing the impact of road disturbances. These shock absorbers play a crucial role in enhancing the driving experience and vehicle safety.</p></p>
