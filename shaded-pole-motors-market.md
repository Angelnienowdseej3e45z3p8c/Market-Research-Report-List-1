<p><p>Shaded pole motors are a type of single-phase induction motor commonly used in small household appliances and fans. The two main types of shaded pole motors are Salient Pole Cover Motor and Hidden Pole Cover Motor. Salient Pole Cover Motors have protruding poles on the stator, while Hidden Pole Cover Motors have concealed poles within the stator. Both types operate on the principle of shading coils to create a phase shift, resulting in a rotating magnetic field and causing the motor to run.</p></p>
