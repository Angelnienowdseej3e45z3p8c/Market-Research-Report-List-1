<p><p>Double-acting pneumatic cylinders are commonly available in two main types: rotary and semi-rotary. Rotary pneumatic cylinders are designed to provide rotational motion in addition to linear motion, making them ideal for applications that require both types of movement. On the other hand, semi-rotary pneumatic cylinders offer limited rotation, typically less than 360 degrees. Both types of cylinders are widely used in industrial automation applications where precise and controlled movements are required.</p></p>
