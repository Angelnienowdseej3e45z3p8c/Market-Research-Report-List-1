<p><p>PH Electrochemical Electrodes Market includes various types such as PH, ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential), Reference, Conductivity, and Dissolved Oxygen electrodes. PH electrodes are used to measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution, ORP electrodes measure the ability of a solution to oxidize or reduce, Reference electrodes provide a stable reference potential, Conductivity electrodes measure the ability of a solution to conduct electricity, and Dissolved Oxygen electrodes measure the amount of oxygen dissolved in a solution. These electrodes cater to different analytical needs in various industries.</p></p>
