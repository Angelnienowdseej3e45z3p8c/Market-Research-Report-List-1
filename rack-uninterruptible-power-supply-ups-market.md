<p><p>Rack Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems come in three main types: Static UPS, Dynamic UPS, and Hybrid UPS. Static UPS systems rely on battery power to provide continuous electricity during outages. Dynamic UPS systems use flywheels or kinetic energy storage for backup power. Hybrid UPS systems combine elements of both static and dynamic systems for optimal performance. Each type caters to different power needs, with static UPS being simpler and more cost-effective, while dynamic and hybrid UPS offer enhanced efficiency and scalability for larger operations.</p></p>
