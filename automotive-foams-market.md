<p><p>Automotive foams are used in various applications within vehicles for insulation, noise reduction, and impact absorption. The main types of automotive foams include polyurethane, polyolefin, polystyrenic, and other specialty foams. Polyurethane foams are versatile and offer excellent cushioning properties. Polyolefin foams are lightweight and offer good thermal insulation. Polystyrenic foams are rigid and provide structural support.
