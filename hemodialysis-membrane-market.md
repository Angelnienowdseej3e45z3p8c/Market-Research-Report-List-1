<p><strong>The Hemodialysis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose Base Membrane</li><li>Synthetic Polymer Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hemodialysis membrane market types can be categorized into cellulose-based membranes and synthetic polymer membranes. Cellulose-based membranes are composed of natural cellulose fibers and have been historically used in hemodialysis due to their biocompatibility and ability to remove toxins effectively. On the other hand, synthetic polymer membranes are made from artificial materials and offer improved performance in terms of selective permeability and biocompatibility. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in the hemodialysis process by facilitating the removal of waste and toxins from the blood.</p></p>
