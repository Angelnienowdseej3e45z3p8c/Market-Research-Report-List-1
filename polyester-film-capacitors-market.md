<p><strong>The Polyester Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polyester Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester film capacitors are categorized into two main types: Plain Polyester Film Capacitors and Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors. Plain Polyester Film Capacitors have film made of pure polyester material, which offers high insulation resistance and excellent dielectric strength. Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors, on the other hand, have a thin metallic film deposited on one or both sides of the polyester film, providing improved conductivity and stability. Both types are commonly used in various electronic applications due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
