<p><strong>The Fruit and Vegetable Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Sorting Machine</li><li>Gravity/Weight Sorting Machine</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fruit and vegetable sorting machines are categorized into three main types based on their sorting mechanisms. Optical sorting machines use cameras and sensors to identify and sort produce based on color, size, and quality. Gravity/weight sorting machines separate fruits and vegetables based on their weight and size as they roll down a conveyor belt. Other sorting machines include machines that use mechanical properties, such as shape and texture, to sort produce. Each type of sorting machine offers unique benefits for efficiently organizing and processing fruits and vegetables.</p></p>
