<p><p>Cheese starter culture market consists of two main types: mesophilic and thermophilic. Mesophilic starter cultures are used in the production of softer cheeses, such as cheddar and brie, at lower temperatures. Thermophilic starter cultures, on the other hand, are used in the production of harder cheeses like parmesan and Swiss at higher temperatures. Each type of culture plays a crucial role in determining the flavor, texture, and quality of the final cheese product.</p></p>
