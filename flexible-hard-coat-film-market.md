<p><p>Flexible hard coat film market can be classified into three main types - TAC, PET, and PEN. TAC (Triacetate Cellulose) films are known for their high optical clarity and excellent durability, making them suitable for protective coatings in electronic displays. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) films are widely used for packaging and labels due to their excellent barrier properties and flexibility. PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) films offer high temperature resistance and dimensional stability, making them ideal for applications in flexible electronics and solar panels.</p></p>
