<p><p>Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) substrate market includes various types such as Precious Metal Sol, Metal Island Membrane Substrate, Rough Electrode, and Solid Nanoparticles. Precious Metal Sol substrates consist of metal nanoparticles dispersed in a solution, Metal Island Membrane Substrates contain metal islands on a dielectric surface, Rough Electrodes have a roughened surface to enhance Raman signal, and Solid Nanoparticles are solid materials with nanoparticles to amplify signals. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the SERS market.</p></p>
