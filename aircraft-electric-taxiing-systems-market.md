<p><p>The Aircraft Electric Taxiing Systems market can be divided into two types: Non-Vehicular Aircraft Electric Green Taxiing Systems (EGTS) and Vehicular Aircraft Electric Green Taxiing Systems (EGTS). Non-Vehicular EGTS refers to systems that are installed on the nose gear of aircraft and use electric power to move the aircraft on the ground. Vehicular EGTS, on the other hand, involves attaching a separate electric vehicle to the aircraft, enabling it to taxi without the use of engines. Both types aim to reduce fuel consumption and emissions during ground operations.</p></p>
