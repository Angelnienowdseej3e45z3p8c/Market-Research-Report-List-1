<p><p>Thermotolerant industrial tapes, such as Teflon Tape and Polyimide Tape, are specialized tapes designed to withstand high temperatures in industrial applications. Teflon Tape, made from polytetrafluoroethylene, is known for its non-stick properties and high temperature resistance. Polyimide Tape, on the other hand, is made from polyimide film and is resistant to heat, chemicals, and abrasion. Both types of tapes are commonly used in industries like electronics, aerospace, and automotive for insulating, masking, and protecting components in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
