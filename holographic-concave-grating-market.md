<p><p>Holographic Concave Grating market is segmented into Transmission Grating and Reflective Grating. Transmission Grating allows light to pass through the grating, creating interference patterns for spectral analysis. Reflective Grating, on the other hand, reflects light back towards the source, providing high efficiency and precise wavelength control. Both types are commonly used in spectroscopy, telecommunications, and laser systems. Transmission Grating is preferred for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while Reflective Grating offers superior performance and accuracy in certain applications.</p></p>
