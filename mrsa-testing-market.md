<p><p>MRSA testing market includes two main types of testing methods: immunodiagnostics and molecular diagnostics. Immunodiagnostics involves detecting MRSA bacteria through the body's immune response, such as by measuring antibodies or antigens. Molecular diagnostics, on the other hand, detect MRSA through its genetic material, usually by amplifying and analyzing specific DNA sequences. Both methods are commonly used in healthcare settings to quickly and accurately diagnose MRSA infections, allowing for timely treatment and prevention of spread.</p></p>
