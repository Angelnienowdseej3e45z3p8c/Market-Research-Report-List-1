<p><strong>The Car Seat Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow Ventilation System</li><li>Suction Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car seat ventilation systems are designed to provide comfort and airflow to passengers in vehicles. The blow ventilation system uses fans or blowers to circulate air through the seat, keeping passengers cool. On the other hand, the suction ventilation system pulls in air from the surrounding cabin and circulates it through the seat, also providing a cooling effect. Both systems aim to improve overall comfort and air circulation within the vehicle, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
