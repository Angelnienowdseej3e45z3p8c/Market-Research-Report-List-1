<p><p>Polymer Modified Cementitious Coatings refer to coatings made by adding polymer materials such as Acrylic Polymer and SBR Latex to cement-based mixtures. Acrylic Polymer is a type of water-based polymer that forms a protective and flexible film on the surface of the coating, offering excellent adhesion and durability. SBR Latex, on the other hand, is a type of synthetic rubber latex that enhances the resilience and waterproofing properties of the coating. Both Acrylic Polymer and SBR Latex are widely used in the market to improve the performance and longevity of cementitious coatings.</p></p>
