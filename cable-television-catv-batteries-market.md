<p><p>Cable Television (CATV) batteries are essential for backup power in case of outages to ensure uninterrupted service. There are two main types of batteries used in CATV systems: Sealed Pure Lead Batteries and Lead Acid Batteries. Sealed Pure Lead Batteries offer higher energy density and longer lifespan, making them ideal for high-performance applications. On the other hand, Lead Acid Batteries are more cost-effective and widely used in CATV systems. Both types of batteries play a crucial role in maintaining reliable power supply for cable television operations.</p></p>
