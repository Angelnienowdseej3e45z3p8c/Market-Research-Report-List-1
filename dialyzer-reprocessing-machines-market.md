Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are designed to perform the entire reprocessing cycle automatically without the need for human intervention. Both types of machines provide a convenient and efficient solution for reprocessing dialyzers, with fully automatic machines offering a higher level of automation and reducing the need for manual labor.</p></p>
