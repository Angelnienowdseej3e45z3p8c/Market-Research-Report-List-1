<p><p>SRAM market is segmented into different types based on specific features and applications. nvSRAM, known for its non-volatility, provides data retention even in the absence of power. Asynchronous SRAM operates independently of clock signals, making it suitable for simpler applications. Synchronous SRAM synchronizes data transfers with clock signals, enabling faster performance. Low Power SRAM is optimized for energy efficiency, extending battery life in mobile devices. Each type caters to different needs in various industries, offering specific advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
