<p><p>NAND Flash market is segmented into MLC NAND and TLC NAND based on the number of bits stored per cell. MLC NAND stores 2 bits per cell, offering a balance between cost and performance. It is commonly used in consumer electronics and enterprise applications. TLC NAND stores 3 bits per cell, providing higher storage capacity at a lower cost compared to MLC NAND. It is suitable for applications where cost-efficiency is a priority, such as in consumer-grade SSDs and USB drives.</p></p>
