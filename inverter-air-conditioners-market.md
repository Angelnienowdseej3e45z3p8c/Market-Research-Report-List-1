<p><p>Inverter air conditioners are categorized into two main types: AC inverter and DC inverter. AC inverter air conditioners use alternating current to regulate the compressor speed, providing more efficient cooling and energy savings. On the other hand, DC inverter air conditioners utilize direct current to control the compressor speed, resulting in even greater energy efficiency and quieter operation. Both types of inverter air conditioners offer significant advantages over traditional non-inverter models in terms of energy consumption and overall performance.</p></p>
