<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LTE Small Base Station Market Types include Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD). TDD involves transmitting and receiving data on the same frequency but at different times, allowing for more flexible use of the spectrum. FDD, on the other hand, uses separate frequencies for uploading and downloading data simultaneously. Both market types cater to different network requirements, with TDD offering greater flexibility and FDD providing more stable connections for voice and data services.</p></p>
