<p><ul><li>General LDPE Film</li><li>Metalized LDPE Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDPE film market consists of general LDPE film and metalized LDPE film. General LDPE film is a versatile packaging material offering excellent moisture resistance, high tensile strength, and flexibility. It is commonly used in various industries including food, pharmaceuticals, and construction. On the other hand, metalized LDPE film is a type of LDPE film that has been coated with a thin layer of metal for enhanced barrier properties and aesthetics. This type of film is often used in packaging applications where barrier protection is crucial, such as in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
