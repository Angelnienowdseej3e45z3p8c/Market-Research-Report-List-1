<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Direct Injection System is available in two types: Solenoid Type and Piezo Type. The Solenoid Type market uses an electromagnetic coil to control the fuel injection process, while the Piezo Type market utilizes piezoelectric crystals to achieve precise fuel injection. The Solenoid Type is more common and cost-effective, while the Piezo Type offers greater precision and efficiency. Both types are widely used in modern diesel engines to optimize fuel consumption and reduce emissions.</p></p>
