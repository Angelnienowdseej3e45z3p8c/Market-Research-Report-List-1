These factors are expected to fuel the growth of the IEC filter market in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>The IEC filter market is segmented into different types based on the number of poles in the filter. 1 pole filters are designed to provide basic electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression for electronic devices. 2 pole filters offer higher EMI suppression capabilities compared to 1 pole filters. Filters with no poles are essentially passive components that offer minimal EMI reduction. There are also other variations of IEC filters available in the market that cater to specific requirements of different electronic applications.</p></p>
