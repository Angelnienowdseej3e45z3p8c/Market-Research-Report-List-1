<p><strong>The Hygiene Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro-Porous Hygiene Films</li><li>Non-Porous Hygiene Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygiene films are typically categorized into two main types: Micro-Porous Hygiene Films and Non-Porous Hygiene Films. Micro-Porous Hygiene Films are breathable films that allow air and moisture to pass through, making them ideal for products like diapers and feminine hygiene products. Non-Porous Hygiene Films, on the other hand, are impermeable films that provide a barrier against liquids and bacteria, commonly used in medical and food packaging applications. Both types of hygiene films play a crucial role in maintaining cleanliness and preventing contamination in various industries.</p></p>
