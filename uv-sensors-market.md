<p><p>UV sensors market can be divided into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA sensors detect long wavelength ultraviolet radiation, which can cause premature skin aging and skin cancer. UVB sensors detect medium wavelength radiation, responsible for sunburns and skin cancer. UVC sensors detect short wavelength radiation, typically filtered out by the Earth's atmosphere but can be harmful if exposure occurs. Each type of UV sensor plays a crucial role in monitoring and protecting against the harmful effects of UV radiation.</p></p>
