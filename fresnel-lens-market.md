<p><strong>The Fresnel Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Imaging Fresnel Lenses</li><li>Non-Imaging Fresnel Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fresnel lenses can be categorized into two main types: imaging Fresnel lenses and non-imaging Fresnel lenses. Imaging Fresnel lenses are used to focus light onto a specific point, making them ideal for applications such as projectors and lighthouses. On the other hand, non-imaging Fresnel lenses are designed to spread light in a uniform manner, making them suitable for tasks like solar energy collection and LED illumination. Both types of Fresnel lenses play crucial roles in various industries where light manipulation is required.</p></p>
