<p><strong>The Gallium Arsenide Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SI GaAS</li><li>SC GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gallium Arsenide Devices Market includes two types: SI GaAs (substrate-insulating GaAs) and SC GaAs (semi-insulating GaAs). SI GaAs devices use an insulating layer to separate the substrate from the active device layers, reducing parasitic capacitance and improving performance. SC GaAs devices have a higher resistivity compared to SI GaAs, making them ideal for high-frequency applications where low noise levels are crucial. Both types bring unique advantages to the market and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
