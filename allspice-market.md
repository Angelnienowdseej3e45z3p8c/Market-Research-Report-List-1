<p><p>Allspice is available in three main market types: Ground Allspice, Whole Allspice, and Rub Allspice. Ground Allspice is the dried and ground berries of the allspice plant, commonly used in baking and cooking. Whole Allspice consists of the dried berries in their whole form, often used in pickling and brining. Rub Allspice is a mixture of ground allspice berries and other spices, used as a seasoning rub for meats and vegetables. Each type offers a unique flavor profile and culinary application.</p></p>
