<p><p>Active sensor market in the anti-lock brake (ABS) sensors industry refers to sensors that require an external power source to function, providing real-time data on wheel speed to prevent wheel lock-up during braking. On the other hand, passive sensor market consists of sensors that do not require an external power source and generate electrical signals in response to the rotation of the wheel. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and efficiency of braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
