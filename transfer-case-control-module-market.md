The TCCM ensures smooth shifting between different driving modes, enhances traction in various road conditions, and improves overall vehicle performance.</p><p>The Transfer Case Control Module Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period. These different types cater to a variety of vehicles and offer options for manufacturers to choose the most suitable module for their specific needs.</p></p>
