<p><p>Single-mode optical fiber patch cords come in various types to suit different connectivity needs. FC type patch cords have a threaded barrel for secure connections, while SC type patch cords have push-pull latching mechanisms for quick installations. ST type patch cords have a bayonet-style connector ideal for industrial applications. LC type patch cords are popular in data centers for their small form factor. Additionally, there are other market types such as MPO/MTP patch cords for high-density applications and simplex/duplex patch cords for different signal transmission requirements.</p></p>
