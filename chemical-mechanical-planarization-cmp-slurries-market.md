<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) slurries are used in semiconductor manufacturing to polish and planarize surfaces. There are two main types of CMP slurries markets: Prestonian Type and Non-Prestonian Type. Prestonian Type slurries have higher abrasiveness and are used for faster removal rates, while Non-Prestonian Type slurries are gentler and used for finer polishing. Both types are essential in achieving smooth and uniform surfaces in semiconductor devices, with their unique characteristics catering to different processing requirements.</p></p>
