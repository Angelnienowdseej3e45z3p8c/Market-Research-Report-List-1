<p><p>The porous polymer bead market can be classified into two main types based on their interaction with solvents: nonpolar and polar. Nonpolar porous polymer beads have low affinity for polar solvents and are mainly used for nonpolar applications such as adsorption of hydrophobic molecules. On the other hand, polar porous polymer beads have high affinity for polar solvents and are utilized for polar applications like separation of polar compounds and removal of metal ions from aqueous solutions. These different types cater to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
