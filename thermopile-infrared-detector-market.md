<p><p>Thermopile Infrared Detector market types include Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor and SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor. Through Hole sensors are mounted by inserting leads in holes on a circuit board, providing stability and robustness. SMD sensors are mounted directly onto the surface of the circuit board, saving space and allowing for automated assembly. Both types offer accurate temperature measurement capabilities and are commonly used in applications such as temperature sensing, gas analysis, and motion detection in various industries.</p></p>
