<p><p>SLT socket market types include LGA type, BGA type, QFN type, and others. LGA type sockets are designed with lands on the socket and pins on the device package. BGA type sockets have balls on the device package and corresponding lands on the socket. QFN type sockets have leads on the bottom surface of the device package. Other types of SLT sockets include various customized designs to accommodate specific requirements of different devices.</p></p>
