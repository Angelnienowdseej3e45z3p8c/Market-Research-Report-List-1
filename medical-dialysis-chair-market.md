<p><p>Manual dialysis chairs are manually adjusted by the healthcare provider to position the patient during dialysis treatment. These chairs are cost-effective and require less maintenance. Electrical dialysis chairs, on the other hand, are equipped with motorized controls for adjusting the position of the patient as well as providing additional comfort features. These chairs are more convenient for both the patient and healthcare provider, but come with a higher cost. Both types of chairs are essential for providing comfortable and effective dialysis treatment to patients.</p></p>
