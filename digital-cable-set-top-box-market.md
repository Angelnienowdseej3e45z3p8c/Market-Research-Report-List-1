<p><p>Digital Cable Set-Top Box market includes different types such as OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT set-top boxes enable users to access streaming services over the internet without the need for a traditional cable or satellite subscription. On the other hand, DTT set-top boxes receive digital television broadcasts through terrestrial signals from antennas. Both types of set-top boxes offer consumers greater flexibility in accessing and watching content, catering to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
