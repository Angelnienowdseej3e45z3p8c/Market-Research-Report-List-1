<p><p>Exterior silicone caulk comes in different types, with two common varieties being waterproof and insulation types. Waterproof silicone caulk is designed to create a watertight seal, making it ideal for outdoor applications where water or moisture may be present. On the other hand, insulation silicone caulk is formulated to provide thermal insulation, helping to prevent heat loss or gain through gaps or cracks in exterior surfaces. Both types offer specialized features to cater to specific needs in the construction and building industry.</p></p>
