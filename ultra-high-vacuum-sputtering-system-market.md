<p><p>Ultra High Vacuum Sputtering Systems are used for depositing thin films on various materials in industries like electronics, aerospace, and automotive. There are two main market types based on system design - Batch Type and Load-Lock Type. Batch Type systems are designed for batch processing multiple samples at once, while Load-Lock Type systems have a separate chamber for loading and unloading samples without breaking the vacuum, allowing for continuous processing. Both types cater to different industrial needs and production requirements.</p></p>
