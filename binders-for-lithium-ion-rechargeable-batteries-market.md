<p><p>Binders for lithium-ion rechargeable batteries are essential components that help hold the active materials together in both the anode and cathode of the battery. Anode binders are used in the anode part of the battery to hold the graphite particles and other components together, ensuring a stable and efficient battery performance. On the other hand, cathode binders are used in the cathode part to enhance the adherence of active materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate, to the current collector, thereby improving the overall battery capacity and durability.</p></p>
