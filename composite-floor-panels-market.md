<p><strong>The Composite Floor Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aluminum Core</li><li>Honeycomb Core</li><li>Foam Core</li><li>Balsa Core</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite floor panels are available in various types, including aluminum core, honeycomb core, foam core, balsa core, and others. Aluminum core panels offer high strength and durability, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Honeycomb core panels are lightweight and have excellent stiffness-to-weight ratio. Foam core panels provide insulation properties and are ideal for temperature-sensitive environments. Balsa core panels offer a combination of strength and lightweight construction. Other types of composite floor panels may include materials like carbon fiber or fiberglass for specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
