<p><ul><li>UV</li><li>DUV</li><li>EUV</li><li>Nanoimprint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithography steppers are used in the semiconductor industry for the production of integrated circuits. There are various types of lithography steppers based on the wavelength of light used in the process. These include UV steppers, DUV steppers, EUV steppers, and Nanoimprint steppers. UV steppers use ultraviolet light, DUV steppers use deep ultraviolet light, EUV steppers use extreme ultraviolet light, and Nanoimprint steppers use a template to imprint patterns on the substrate. Each type of lithography stepper has its own advantages and applications.</p></p>
