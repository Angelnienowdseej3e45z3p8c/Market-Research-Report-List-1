<p><strong>The Smart Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear Sortation Systems are conveyor systems that move items in a straight line, allowing for high-speed sorting of items in a single direction. Loop Sortation Systems are conveyor systems that loop items around in a continuous circuit, allowing for more complex sorting patterns and higher throughput. Both systems are used in the Smart Sorting Machine market to efficiently sort items in warehouses, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities. Linear Sortation Systems are ideal for applications with limited space, while Loop Sortation Systems are better suited for larger facilities with higher sorting volumes.</p></p>
