<p><p>Flat Belt Rotocure Machine and Triangle Belt Rotocure Machine are two types of machines used in the rubber rotocure process. Flat Belt Rotocure Machine utilizes a flat belt to transfer heat and pressure to the rubber compound, resulting in a smooth and uniform product. On the other hand, Triangle Belt Rotocure Machine uses a triangle belt for the same purpose, offering flexibility and efficiency in the production process. Both machines play a crucial role in the rubber industry by ensuring high-quality and consistent output.</p></p>
