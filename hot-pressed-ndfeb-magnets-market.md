<p><p>Hot-pressed NdFeB magnets are divided into two main market types: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic magnets have uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them suitable for applications where magnetization needs to occur in multiple directions. Anisotropic magnets, on the other hand, are magnetized along a preferred direction, offering higher magnetic performance but limited orientation flexibility. Both types cater to specific market demands, with isotropic magnets providing versatility and anisotropic magnets offering enhanced performance for specialized applications.</p></p>
