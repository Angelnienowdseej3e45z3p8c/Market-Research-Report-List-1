<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) consist of several types designed for different storage and retrieval needs. Vertical Lift Modules utilize vertical space efficiently, Carousels rotate for easy access, Mid Load systems are a combination of unit/load and mini/load systems, and Autostore systems use robots for item storage and retrieval. Each type offers unique advantages based on the size and weight of items being stored.</p></p>
