Roller conveyors use rollers to move objects, while belt conveyors use belts. Overhead conveyors are mounted on ceilings for efficient movement of goods. Pallet conveyors are used for handling palletized goods. Other conveyors include spiral conveyors, chain conveyors, and modular conveyors. Each type offers specific advantages in terms of efficiency, flexibility, and customization for different intralogistics applications.</p></p>
