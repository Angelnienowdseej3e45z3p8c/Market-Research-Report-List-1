<p><p>Tahini sauce is available in two main types in the market - Hulled Tahini and Unhulled Tahini. Hulled Tahini is made from sesame seeds that have had their outer shell removed, resulting in a smoother and milder flavor. On the other hand, Unhulled Tahini is made from sesame seeds with their shell intact, providing a stronger and nuttier taste. Both types have their own unique characteristics and are used in various cuisines around the world.</p></p>
