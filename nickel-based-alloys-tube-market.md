<p><p>Nickel Based Alloys Tubes are available in two main types: Seamless Tubes and Welded Tubes. Seamless Tubes are made from a single piece of metal and have no joints or seams, providing a smooth and continuous surface. Welded Tubes, on the other hand, are made by welding together multiple pieces of metal. While Seamless Tubes are known for their high strength and corrosion resistance, Welded Tubes are more cost-effective and versatile in terms of customization options.</p></p>
