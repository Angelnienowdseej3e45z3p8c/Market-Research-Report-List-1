<p><strong>The Industrial Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Systems</li><li>Local Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central systems in the industrial air conditioning market refer to large-scale systems that are used to cool entire buildings or facilities. These systems typically consist of a central unit that distributes cooled air through ductwork to different areas. On the other hand, local systems are smaller-scale units that are used to cool specific spaces or rooms within a building. These systems are often more flexible and can be installed quickly to meet specific cooling needs. Both central and local systems play a crucial role in maintaining indoor comfort and productivity in industrial settings.</p></p>
