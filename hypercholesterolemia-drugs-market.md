<p><strong>The Hypercholesterolemia Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Statins</li><li>Non-Statins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hypercholesterolemia drugs market can be classified into two main types: statins and non-statins. Statins are the most commonly prescribed drugs for managing high cholesterol levels. They work by inhibiting enzymes involved in cholesterol production. Non-statins, on the other hand, include a variety of medications like bile acid sequestrants, cholesterol absorption inhibitors, and PCSK9 inhibitors. These drugs work by different mechanisms, such as preventing cholesterol absorption from the intestine or reducing LDL receptor degradation. Both statins and non-statins contribute to the treatment of hypercholesterolemia, targeting different aspects of cholesterol metabolism.</p></p>
