<p><p>High-gluten flour is available in two main market types: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced by industrial machinery which grinds the wheat into fine powder, resulting in a consistent texture and high gluten content. On the other hand, stone ground flour is made by grinding the wheat between two large stones, preserving more of the wheat's natural flavor and nutrients. Both types of flour have their own unique characteristics and are used in various culinary applications.</p></p>
