<p><p>Dispersing coating additives are classified based on the type of medium in which they are dispersed. Water-based additives are dispersed in water and are environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based additives are dispersed in solvents and provide enhanced performance but may have higher VOC levels. Powder-based additives are dispersed in powder form and offer good chemical resistance and durability. Each type of dispersing coating additive has its own advantages and applications, catering to different customer needs and preferences.</p></p>
