<p><p>Reactive dye inks are mainly used for natural textiles like cotton and silk, offering vibrant and long-lasting colors. Acidic inks are used for synthetic fibers and have good wash fastness properties. Paint inks are used for printing on dark fabrics and offer a wide color gamut. Dispersion inks are suited for printing on a variety of substrates, while sublimation inks are ideal for polyester fabrics and create a permanent bond with the fibers without leaving a visible layer on the surface.</p></p>
