<p><p>N-type bifacial solar cells are gaining traction in the market with various types such as HJT cell, TOPCon cell, and others. HJT cells, which stand for heterojunction with intrinsic thin layer, offer high efficiency by combining amorphous and crystalline silicon. TOPCon cells utilize passivating contacts for improved efficiency. Other types of N-type cells may include PERC cells, IBC cells, and HIT cells, each with its own unique technology and advantages in the bifacial solar cell market.</p></p>
