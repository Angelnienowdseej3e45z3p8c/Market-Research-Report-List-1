<p><p>Mesh Liquid Filter Bags come in two main types: Monofilament Mesh and Multifilament Mesh. Monofilament Mesh bags are made from single strand fibers, providing a smooth surface for efficient filtration of fine particles. Multifilament Mesh bags are made from multiple intertwined fibers, offering a higher dirt-holding capacity and better removal of larger particles. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications for liquid filtration, with Monofilament Mesh bags suited for finer filtration and Multifilament Mesh bags ideal for higher dirt loads.</p></p>
