<p><p>BeiDou Navigation Satellite System (BDS) includes two main market types: Constellations and Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS). Constellations refer to the group of satellites that make up the BDS system and provide positioning, navigation, and timing services. SBAS, on the other hand, are ground-based systems that improve the accuracy, integrity, and availability of signals provided by the BDS constellations. Both types of markets play a crucial role in enhancing navigation capabilities and providing accurate positioning information globally.</p></p>
