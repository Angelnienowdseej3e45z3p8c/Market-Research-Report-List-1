<p><p>Omega-3 ingredients market includes two main types: marine omega-3 and algae omega-3. Marine omega-3 is sourced from fish oil, primarily from fatty fish like salmon, mackerel, and sardines. Algae omega-3, on the other hand, is sourced from algae, making it a plant-based alternative for those who follow a vegetarian or vegan diet. Both marine and algae omega-3 are rich in EPA (eicosapentaenoic acid) and DHA (docosahexaenoic acid), essential fatty acids known for their numerous health benefits, including supporting heart and brain health.</p></p>
