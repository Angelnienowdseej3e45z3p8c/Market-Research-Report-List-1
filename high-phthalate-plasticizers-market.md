<p><p>High phthalate plasticizers market is segmented into various types such as DINP, DIDP, DPHP, DIUP, and DTDP. DINP (diisononyl phthalate) is widely used in PVC products due to its flexibility and durability. DIDP (diisodecyl phthalate) is known for its low volatility and good heat stability. DPHP (Dipropylene glycol monomethyl ether) is commonly used in flooring and non-PVC applications. DIUP (diisobutyl phthalate) offers good stability and low volatility, making it suitable for automotive and construction industries. DTDP (diisotridecyl phthalate) is used as a processing aid in plastic production.</p></p>
