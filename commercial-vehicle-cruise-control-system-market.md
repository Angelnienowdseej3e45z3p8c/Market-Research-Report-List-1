<p><p>The commercial vehicle cruise control system market can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic control and full automatic control. Semi-automatic control requires some level of human input, such as setting the desired speed, while the system helps maintain it. On the other hand, full automatic control takes over complete control of the vehicle's speed, acceleration, and braking without any human intervention. Both types offer increased convenience, efficiency, and safety for commercial vehicle operators.</p></p>
