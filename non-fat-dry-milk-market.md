<p><p>Non Fat Dry Milk can be categorized into three different market types based on the heat treatment it undergoes during processing: Low-Heat, Medium-Heat, and High-Heat. Low-Heat Non Fat Dry Milk is processed at a lower temperature, preserving more of the milk's natural flavor and nutrients. Medium-Heat Non Fat Dry Milk undergoes a slightly higher temperature processing, which results in a slightly different flavor profile. High-Heat Non Fat Dry Milk is processed at a high temperature, resulting in a more caramelized flavor and darker color compared to the other two types.</p></p>
