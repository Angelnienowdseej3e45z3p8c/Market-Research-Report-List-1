<p><p>3D Printing Plastic and Photopolymer Material market includes various types such as Fused Deposition Modelling (FDM), Stereolithography (SLA), Polyjet Printing, Laser Sintering, Digital Light Processing, and Others. FDM involves melting and extruding plastic filament to create objects layer by layer. SLA uses UV light to cure liquid resin into solid objects. Polyjet Printing creates detailed, multi-material objects by jetting photopolymer droplets. Laser Sintering fuses powdered materials with a laser to build objects. Digital Light Processing uses light to cure liquid resin into solid parts.</p></p>
