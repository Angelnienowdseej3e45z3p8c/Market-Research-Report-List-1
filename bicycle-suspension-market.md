<p><p>Bicycle suspension refers to the system of springs, shock absorbers, and linkages that connect the wheels to the frame of a bicycle. This system is designed to absorb impacts and vibrations from uneven terrain, providing a smoother and more comfortable ride for the cyclist. Suspension systems can be found on various types of bicycles, including mountain bikes, road bikes, and hybrid bikes.</p><p>The Bicycle Suspension Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period. The increasing popularity of mountain biking and the growing trend of using bicycles for recreational purposes are driving the demand for bicycles with advanced suspension systems. Manufacturers are constantly innovating to improve the performance and efficiency of bicycle suspensions, with a focus on enhancing ride comfort, control, and durability.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Bicycle Suspension Market is the integration of electronic suspension systems, which use sensors and actuators to automatically adjust the suspension settings based on riding conditions. This technology allows cyclists to enjoy a more personalized and optimized riding experience. Additionally, lightweight materials such as carbon fiber are being increasingly used in the construction of bicycle suspension components, making them more durable and efficient.</p></p>
