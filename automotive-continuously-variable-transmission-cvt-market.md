<p><strong>The Automotive Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive continuously variable transmission (CVT) market includes two main types: belt-chain drive CVT and planetary CVT. Belt-chain drive CVT uses a system of belts and pulleys to provide seamless gear shifting, while planetary CVT uses a complex system of gears to achieve the same result. Belt-chain CVTs are more common in smaller vehicles due to their cost-effectiveness, while planetary CVTs are often found in larger vehicles for their higher efficiency and durability. Both types offer smoother driving experience and improved fuel efficiency compared to traditional transmission systems.</p></p>
