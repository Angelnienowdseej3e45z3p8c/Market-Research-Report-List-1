<p><p>Pharmaceutical AGVs come in three main market types: Unit Load Type, Automated Forklift Type, and Tugger Type. Unit Load Type AGVs are designed to transport entire pallets or large containers of pharmaceutical products. Automated Forklift Type AGVs are equipped with forklift arms to lift and move individual items within a pharmaceutical warehouse or manufacturing facility. Tugger Type AGVs are designed to pull carts or trailers filled with pharmaceutical products around a facility. Each market type offers specific benefits and functionalities to streamline pharmaceutical operations.</p></p>
