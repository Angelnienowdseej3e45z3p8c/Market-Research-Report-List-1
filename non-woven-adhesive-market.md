<p><p>The non-woven adhesive market includes various types of adhesives, namely SBC-based adhesives and APAO-based adhesives. SBC-based (Styrene Block Copolymer) adhesives are widely used due to their excellent adhesion and bonding properties. They are commonly utilized in diaper construction, medical products, and hygiene applications. On the other hand, APAO-based (Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefin) adhesives offer excellent thermal stability and are extensively employed in automotive, textiles, and packaging industries. Both adhesive types cater to the needs of different sectors, ensuring reliable bonding in non-woven applications.</p></p>
