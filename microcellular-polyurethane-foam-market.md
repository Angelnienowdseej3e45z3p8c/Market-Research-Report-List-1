<p><p>Microcellular polyurethane foam is classified into two main types based on density: low density foam and high density foam. Low density foam has a lighter weight and is commonly used for applications where flexibility and cushioning are important, such as in furniture and bedding. On the other hand, high density foam is heavier and offers more durability and support, making it suitable for applications that require strength and rigidity, such as in industrial machinery and automotive components. Both types of foam offer unique properties and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
