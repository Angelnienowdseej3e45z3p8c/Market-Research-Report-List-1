<p><ul><li>Inorganic Terrazzo Tile</li><li>Epoxy Terrazzo Tile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terrazzo tile market can be divided into two main types: inorganic terrazzo tile and epoxy terrazzo tile. </p><p>Inorganic terrazzo tile is made from a mixture of cement, marble chips, and pigments, resulting in a durable and low-maintenance option for flooring. </p><p>On the other hand, epoxy terrazzo tile uses epoxy resin as a binding agent, offering a more flexible and customizable design option. Epoxy terrazzo tile is known for its seamless installation process and high resistance to stains and chemicals. Both types of terrazzo tiles are popular choices for commercial and residential flooring projects.</p></p>
