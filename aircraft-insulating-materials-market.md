<p><p>Aircraft insulating materials market is categorized into three main types based on their functions: thermal insulation, acoustic & vibration insulation, and electric insulation. Thermal insulation helps regulate the temperature inside the aircraft, protecting it from extreme heat or cold. Acoustic & vibration insulation reduces noise and vibrations within the aircraft cabin for a more comfortable flight experience. Electric insulation is essential for protecting the aircraft's electrical components from damage and ensuring safe operation. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and safety of the aircraft.</p></p>
