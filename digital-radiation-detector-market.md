<p><p>The digital radiation detector market is segmented into three main types: Gas Ionization Detectors, Semiconductor Detectors, and Scintillation Detectors. Gas Ionization Detectors utilize gas-filled chambers to detect radiation levels accurately. Semiconductor Detectors use semiconductors to directly convert radiation into electrical signals for precise measurements. Scintillation Detectors use scintillating materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, which is then converted into electronic signals. Each type of detector offers unique benefits and applications in the field of radiation detection and monitoring.</p></p>
