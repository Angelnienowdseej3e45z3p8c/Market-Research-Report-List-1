<p><p>Silicone rubber for electronic applications is primarily divided into two main markets: Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) and High Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV). RTV silicone rubber is typically used for electronic encapsulation, potting, and sealing due to its ability to cure at room temperature and its excellent flexibility and resistance to moisture. HTV silicone rubber, on the other hand, is designed for high-temperature applications such as insulating cables and wires, as it can withstand extreme temperatures without losing its properties. Both types of silicone rubber play crucial roles in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
