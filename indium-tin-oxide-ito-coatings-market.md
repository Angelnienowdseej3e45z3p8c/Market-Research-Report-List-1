<p><p>Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) coatings are categorized into two main types based on purity levels: high purity and low purity. High purity ITO coatings are made with a higher level of indium and tin, ensuring better conductivity and transparency. These coatings are commonly used in high-tech applications like touch screens and solar panels. On the other hand, low purity ITO coatings have a lower concentration of indium and tin, making them more cost-effective but less efficient in terms of conductivity and transparency.</p></p>
