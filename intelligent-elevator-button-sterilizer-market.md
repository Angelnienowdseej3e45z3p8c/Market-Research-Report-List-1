<p><p>Intelligent Elevator Button Sterilizer markets consist of two types - Internal and External. Internal Elevator Button Sterilizers are installed within the elevator cab and dispense sanitizer directly onto the buttons, ensuring a clean and hygienic touch surface for passengers. External Elevator Button Sterilizers, on the other hand, are attached outside the elevator near the buttons, offering a convenient way for users to sanitize their hands before and after touching the buttons. Both types aim to improve public health by minimizing the spread of germs in high-traffic areas.</p></p>
