<p><p>Ferrous metal recycling equipment market consists of various types of equipment such as sorting systems, metal shredders, briquetting machines, granulating machines, and other specialized machinery. Sorting systems are used to separate different types of metals, while metal shredders break down large pieces of metal into smaller fragments. Briquetting machines compact metal scraps into denser forms for easier transportation and storage. Granulating machines process metal scraps into granules for melting or other purposes. Other equipment in the market includes magnetic separators, conveyors, and scrap balers.</p></p>
