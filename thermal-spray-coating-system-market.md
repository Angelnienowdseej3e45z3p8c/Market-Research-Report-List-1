<p><strong>The Thermal Spray Coating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame Spray</li><li>Electric Arc Wire Spray</li><li>Plasma Spray</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal spray coating systems are primarily categorized into four types: Flame Spray, Electric Arc Wire Spray, Plasma Spray, and Others. Flame Spray involves the combustion of gases to melt a coating material and spray it onto a surface. Electric Arc Wire Spray uses an electric arc to melt wire materials for coating. Plasma Spray utilizes a high-temperature plasma jet to melt and propel coating material onto a surface. Other types of thermal spray systems may include High-Velocity Oxygen Fuel (HVOF) and Detonation Gun (D-Gun) systems.</p></p>
