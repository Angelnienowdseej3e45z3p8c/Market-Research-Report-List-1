<p><p>Circulating oils market can be categorized into two main types based on viscosity: high viscosity and low viscosity oils. High viscosity oils are thicker and flow more slowly, providing better lubrication and protection for heavy-duty machinery and equipment. On the other hand, low viscosity oils are lighter and flow more easily, making them suitable for use in smaller machinery and equipment where precision and speed are important. Both types of oils play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operation and extending the lifespan of industrial equipment.</p></p>
