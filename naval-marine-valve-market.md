<p><p>Naval marine valves are categorized into four main types based on their structure and functionality. Straight-through valves allow fluid to flow straight through without any obstruction. Once-through valves have a mechanism that allows the fluid to pass through only once. Angle type valves have an angled design for specific applications. Plunger valves use a plunger to regulate the flow of fluid. Each type of valve is used in different marine applications based on their design and functionality.</p></p>
