<p><strong>The Grease Hydraulic Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Positive-Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grease hydraulic pumps market consists of two main types: piston pump and positive-displacement pump. </p><p>Piston pumps use reciprocating pistons to pressurize the grease and deliver it through the system. They are known for their high efficiency and precise control.</p><p>Positive-displacement pumps, on the other hand, deliver a fixed amount of grease with each revolution. They are suitable for applications where a constant flow rate is required.</p><p>Both types play a crucial role in various industries where precise grease lubrication is necessary.</p></p>
