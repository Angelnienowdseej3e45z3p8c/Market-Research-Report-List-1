<p><p>High Temperature Lubricant & Grease Market can be categorized into two main types, Soap Thickener and Non-Soap Thickener. Soap Thickener greases are made from metallic soaps and have excellent water resistance and shear stability. Non-Soap Thickener greases, on the other hand, are made from inorganic materials like clay or silica. These greases offer better high-temperature performance and compatibility with a wider range of materials. Both types of greases have their own advantages and are used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
