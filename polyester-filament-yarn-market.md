<p><p>Polyester Filament Yarn market consists of various types such as Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), Polyester Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY), and Polyester Drawn Textured Yarn (DTY). POY is pre-oriented yarn that can be directly used in texturizing process. FDY is fully oriented yarn with high tenacity and uniformity, suitable for use in apparel and home textiles. DTY is textured yarn with a crimped appearance, ideal for creating fabric with a natural look and feel. Additionally, there are other specialized markets for polyester filament yarn catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
