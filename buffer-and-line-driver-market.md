<p><strong>The Buffer and Line Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three States</li><li>Open Drain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buffer and line driver markets can be classified into three states: high, low, and high impedance. In the high state, the output signal is at a high voltage level, while in the low state, the output signal is at a low voltage level. In the high impedance state, the output signal is electrically isolated from the rest of the circuit. Open drain market refers to a type of buffer or line driver where the output signal is connected to ground when in the low state, allowing for easy interfacing with other devices.</p></p>
