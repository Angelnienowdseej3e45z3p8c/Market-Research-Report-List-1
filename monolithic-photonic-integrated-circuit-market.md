<p><p>Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit Market consists of two main types: Passive Photonic Integrated Circuits and Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. </p><p>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuits do not require external power sources and primarily perform functions like splitting, combining, and filtering light signals. They are essential components in optical communication systems.</p><p>Active Photonic Integrated Circuits, on the other hand, utilize external power sources to manipulate and control light signals. They play a crucial role in amplifying and modulating optical signals in various applications such as telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
